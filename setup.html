🌻 Garden Care

Set it up on your phone — about 5 minutes.

Hi! This is the little garden app I made for us. It gives you a plant-care schedule tuned to your climate and sends the reminders right to your phone.

⚠️ Do this first: text or email me the Gmail address you'll use (the Google account on your phone). I have to give your account access once, or the reminder step will say "access blocked." Wait until I say "you're added" before Step 5.

① Open the app in the right browser

  • Android: open Chrome and go to the link below.
  • iPhone/iPad: open Safari (not Chrome) and go to the same link.
Open Garden Care →

❗ Don't use Firefox — the Google reminder part won't work in it.

② Add it to your home screen

  • Android (Chrome): tap the ⋮ menu (top-right) → Add to Home screen → Add.
  • iPhone (Safari): tap Share (box with ↑) → Add to Home Screen → Add.
Then open it from the new 🌻 Garden Care icon.

③ Set your climate zone

Pick your USDA hardiness zone (not sure? Google "USDA hardiness zone" + your zip, or ask me). Region/notes are optional. Tap Save & pick plants.

④ Add your plants

Tap the checkbox next to each plant you have (there's a search box up top), then Add selected plants. You can add more anytime from the Add tab.

⑤ Turn on phone reminders (after I've said you're added)

  1. Tap the ⚙ Settings tab (bottom-right).
  2. Tap Connect Google account.
  3. Choose the same Gmail you sent me.
  4. You'll see a scary screen: "Google hasn't verified this app." That's normal for a private family app. Tap Advanced → Go to Garden Care (unsafe). It is not unsafe — that's just Google's wording for apps that aren't in an app store.
  5. Tap Continue / Allow.
  6. Back in the app, tap Sync now. You'll see "Synced: …".

⑥ See your reminders

Open the Google Tasks app (or Google Calendar → Tasks) and find the "Garden Care" list. Your tasks are there, grouped by Saturday. Check them off as you go! 🌱

Using it day to day

  • 🌻 icon = browse plants, see care info, add/edit.
  • Google Tasks app = your actual reminders.
  • Changed plants? Open Garden Care → Settings → Sync now to refresh.
  • Your garden is saved on your phone. To back up / move phones: Settings → Export, then Import on the other device.

If something goes wrong

  • "Access blocked / not a tester" → I haven't added your Gmail yet, or you picked a different account. Send me the exact address.
  • Sign-in screen blank / nothing happens → you're probably in Firefox. Use Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone).
  • App looks out of date → close it completely and reopen.
  • Still stuck? Text me a screenshot. 💛
